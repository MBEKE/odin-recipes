Fish Wet Fry

Description

To prepare fish wet fry, start by cleaning 2–3 whole fish or fillets thoroughly and pat them
dry. Season the fish with salt, black pepper, and optional spices like paprika or turmeric.
Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat and fry the fish until golden brown on both sides,
then set aside. In the same skillet, sauté chopped onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
Add diced tomatoes and cook until they form a thick sauce. Optional ingredients like
diced bell peppers, chili, or fresh coriander can be added for extra flavor

Return the fried fish to the skillet and gently coat it with the sauce, simmering for 5–10
minutes to allow the flavors to blend. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
Serve hot with ugali, rice, or your favorite side dish. Enjoy the rich, savory flavors of this,
quick and satisfying dish!

Ingredients

  • Goat meat - 2 lbs, bone-in, cut into chunks
  • Salt - 2-3 table spoons
  • Black Paper - 1-2 teapoons
  • Garlic Clover - 4-6 minced/whole for stuffing
  • Fresh herbs - Rosemary, thyme, and sage (a handful, chopped or whole)
  • Onion - 1 large, quatered for stuffing
  • Potatoes - 3, peeled and cubed
  • Butter - 1/2 cup, melted
  • Vegetable oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Optional - paprika or other spices for additional seasoning
