Stew Goat

Description

To make stewed goat, season 2 lbs of goat meat with salt, pepper, and spices, then brown
in a pot with oil. Remove and sauté onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant, then add
diced tomatoes and tomato paste, cooking until thickened. Return the goat meat, add
broth or water, thyme, bay leaves, and optional scotch bonnet pepper, then simmer for
1.5–2 hours until tender. Add carrots and potatoes, cook for 20–30 minutes, and adjust
seasoning before serving with rice or bread. Enjoy the rich, hearty flavors!

Ingredients

  • Goat meat - 2 lbs, bone-in, cut into chunks
  • Salt - 2-3 table spoons
  • Black Paper - 1-2 teapoons
  • Garlic Clover - 4-6 minced/whole for stuffing
  • Fresh herbs - Rosemary, thyme, and sage (a handful, chopped or whole)
  • Onion - 1 large, quatered for stuffing
  • Potatoes - 3, peeled and cubed
  • Butter - 1/2 cup, melted
  • Vegetable oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Optional - paprika or other spices for additional seasoning
