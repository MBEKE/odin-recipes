Roasted Turkey

Description

A roasted turkey is a classic dish perfect for special occasions.
Start by cleaning and drying the turkey, then season it with a blend
of salt, pepper, garlic, and herbs. Stuff the cavity with aromatics
like onions, garlic, and lemons, and brush the skin with a butter-herb
mixture. Roast the turkey at 325°F (165°C) on a rack, basting every 30
minutes, until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C). Let it rest for
20–30 minutes before carving for a juicy, flavorful centerpiece.

Ingredients

  • Turkey - 1 whole (size depending on your needs)
  • Salt - 2-3 table spoons
  • Black Paper - 1-2 teapoons
  • Garlic Clover - 4-6 minced/whole for stuffing
  • Fresh herbs - Rosemary, thyme, and sage (a handful, chopped or whole)
  • Onion - 1 large, quatered for stuffing
  • Lemon - 1 large, halved for stuffing
  • Butter - 1/2 cup, melted
  • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons
  • Optional - paprika or other spices for additional seasoning
